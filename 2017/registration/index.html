Registration process

Registration for TJ IOI 2017 closed on Wednesday, April 19, 2017. If you werenâ€™t able to register, we hope to see you next year!

Part 1. Team registration

  1. First, complete the following registration form. Make sure that you enter your email correctly, as we will use it to contact you. We strongly prefer that a teacher or school official fill out the form.

    Registration form

  2. Once you submit the form, you will automatically receive an email with your responses and a link to edit your responses. You may use this link to update information for your team, including the team name or students, before the registration deadline.

  3. Next, send a check for the registration fee of $50, payable to TJHSST, addressed as follows:

    Attn: Thomas Rudwick
    TJHSST
    6560 Braddock Road
    Alexandria, VA 22312

  4. Once we receive your check, we will inform you via email. We will not cash your check until we have reserved a spot for your team.

Part 2. Before the competition

After the registration deadline on April 19, if there are any changes to your team, notify us by email at tjioiofficers@gmail.com as soon as possible. Note that we may not be able to accomodate changes in T-shirt size.

We will provide more details to registered teams via email as we approach the competition. In addition, please note the following important links:

  1. Each student is required to complete the Media Release Form (pdf). This can either be sent in the mail, scanned and sent by e-mail, or brought in person on the day of the competition. Additionally, make sure that your students complete any other forms required by your school or school district.

  2. Ask your students to complete the Student Information Form. This will help us create a better competition this year and in the future.

  3. We encourage your team to review the 2017 Study Guide (pdf) to prepare for the competition. Please keep in touch with us about how you're using our materials!
