 News

Registration for TJ IOI 2017 is now open! Registration will close on Saturday, April 15, 2017.

Clarification: TJ IOI is not open to students from TJHSST itself (unlike, for instance, HackTJ). We need TJ students to write and test contest problems, as well as help out on the day of the contest.

 Schedule (Tentative)

TJ IOI 2017 will be held on Saturday, March 13. The following schedule for the day of the competition is subject to change, but it should be reasonably close to the actual schedule.

8:30 – 9:00 Registration
9:00 – 9:45 Opening Ceremony
10:00 – 11:00 Written Round
11:00 – 11:45 Practice Programming Contest
11:45 – 12:45 Lunch
1:00 – 4:00 Programming Round
4:15 – 5:00 Closing and Awards

 Logistics

TJ IOI will be held at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. We will send information about parking and rooms to team coaches as the day of the contest approaches.
