FAQ

  • What is TJIOI?
    TJIOI is a contest for people interested in Computer Science.
  • When is TJIOI?
    To be completely honest, we don't know either at this moment in time. Time is merely a social construct and it can be Saturday forever.
  • How can I prepare?
    We plan to have study materials available by mid-January.
  • Who is eligible participate in TJ IOI?

    High schools in Northern Virginia, or in close proximity, may participate in TJ IOI. Each high school may send a maximum of three teams, each of which may have a maximum of four people. Middle schools will be considered on a case-by-case basis.

  • What programming languages may I use at TJ IOI?

    Competitors will be able to use C/C++, Python 2/3, or Java.

  • What should I bring to TJ IOI?

    Lunch will be provided. For the programming section, computers will also be provided. Each team will share one computer. Feel free to bring any printed material that you like; however, access to online resources will be prohibited.
